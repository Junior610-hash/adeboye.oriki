ORÍKÌ ÀSÀMU

  • Àsàmu,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí a bí kì í bẹ̀rù ogun,
  • Ọmọ akọni tí ń ṣeré nínú iná,
  • Ọmọ tí kì í jẹ́ kí ọ̀ta kọ́ lójú.
  • Àsàmu, ẹ̀dun ọlá tó wà lórí orí,
  • Ọmọ ológo tó jókòó sára ọlá,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí orí rere bọ̀ sìn bí ajá ọba.
  • Àsàmu, ọmọ ẹni tí ogun kì í bọ̀ lórí,
  • Ọmọ akíkanjú tí ń jẹ́ kí òrò di ṣeré ní ọwọ́ rẹ̀,
  • Ọmọ tí kì í bẹ̀rù ẹ̀dá bí ó tilẹ̀ jẹ́ ẹni tí wọ́n bẹ̀rù.
  • Ẹni tí Ọlọ́run gbé dákẹ́, kò sí ẹni tí yó lè gbé wọ́,
  • Ọmọ tí ojú rere ń tẹ̀ rẹ̀ lé,
  • Tí ayé ń fẹ́ kí o ṣegun gbogbo ibi.
  • Àsàmu, ọmọ oloye tó mọ orí ẹ̀mí,
  • Ọmọ tó ní irúgbẹ̀ olójú-inú,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí wọ́n kó sípò ọlá kí a lè mọ pé ọba ló bí i.
  • Ẹ̀mí tí kì í ronú àìlera,
  • Ọmọ ìyanu ayé tí adúrà fẹ́ran,
  • Adé ẹ̀mí tó bẹ̀rẹ̀ ní kíkún ayé.
  • Àsàmu,
  • Kí orí rẹ̀ má ṣe bà jẹ́,
  • Kí o má bà owó tà,
  • Kí ibi má bà ẹ́ mọ́lẹ̀,
  • Kí ayé sì dá ọ sí rere gbogbo ọjọ́ ayé rẹ.
  • Àsàmu ọmọ akọ́ni,
  • Akin ni wọ́n bí,
  • Akin ni wọ́n kọ́,
  • Ọmọ tí a gbé sókè kò ní ṣubú sálẹ̀.

    • ENGLISH TRANSLATION / MEANING

  • Àsàmu,
  • Child born without fear of battle,
  • A brave one who plays in the midst of fire,
  • One who never allows the enemy to triumph.
  • Àsàmu, the proud thunder of royalty upon the head,
  • Child of glory seated in honor,
  • One whose destiny follows him like a king’s loyal guard.
  • Àsàmu, a child whom battles cannot overwhelm,
  • A courageous one who turns challenges into toys in his hands,
  • One who fears no one, yet everyone fears him.
  • One whom God has elevated — no man can pull down,
    • A child followed by favor, Whom the world desires to see victorious.
  • Àsàmu, noble-born who understands the spiritual crown,
  • A child gifted with deep inner sight,
  • Born into honor so all may know royalty gave him life.
  • A spirit that never thinks of weakness,
  • A marvel that prayers adore,
  • A crown of destiny built on fullness and greatness.
  • Àsàmu,
  • May your destiny never spoil,
  • May your wealth never scatter,
  • May evil never overpower you,
  • May your life be blessed in every day of your journey.
  • Àsàmu, child of a warrior,
  • Born brave,
  • Trained brave,
  • One lifted high who shall never fall.

ABENI


  • Meaning: We begged to have this child (highly desired or an answer to prayers).
  • Yoruba Oríkì
  • Àbẹ̀ní, Àbá-tí-bẹ̀bẹ̀-fún.
  • Àbẹ̀ní, Ọmọ ìyáàmi-òrò.
  • Alábọ́sí-tìrẹ̀.
  • Àbẹ̀ní, ọkùnrin l'obìrin.
  • A-yẹ́-ni-s'ọ̀rọ̀, a-yẹ́-ni-f'ẹnu-yọ́.
  • Oníwà pè̩lú, onírínú dídùn.
  • Ọmọ onígun mẹ́fà, onígun méjìlá.
  • A-rẹwà-lójú-ayé.

    • English Meaning:

  • Abeni, the one we begged for to have.
  • Abeni, child of my divine mother (goddess).
  • Abeni, who has her own hiding place (secretive, reserved, dignified).
  • Abeni, a man in a woman's body (strong/brave).
  • One who befriends you to speak to you, one who befriends you to make you happy with her mouth.
  • The owner of a calm character, a pleasant disposition.
  • Child of the six corners, owner of twelve corners (a complete, complex person).
  • The beautiful one in the eyes of the world.

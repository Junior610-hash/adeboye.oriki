Oriki of the Ooni of Ife (Òòni ti Ilé-Ifẹ̀) and their Meaning

  • Ooni Ọ̀rúnmìlà àgbẹ̀rù òkè, ẹni tí Ọlọ́run gbé lókè àiyé
  • Ọba Òòni, Aláṣẹ Ilẹ̀-Ifẹ̀
  • Adé ò ní rọ̀ lórí rẹ
  • Ọmọ Odùduwà, Olúwa Ilé-Ifẹ̀
  • Ọba tí wọ́n bí ní ọwọ́ ayé, tí wọ́n ṣe ní ọwọ́ ọ̀run
  • Ará Ìfe, a gún régé régé ní ilé ayé
  • Kábíyèsí Ọọ̀ni, Aláyélúwà
  • Onímọ̀lẹ̀ tí ń bó Òrìṣà wọlé
  • Ọba tí ń gbéni ní’jà, tí kì í ṣeni níyà
  • Arole Odùduwà, Akọni tí kì í ṣegbé
  • Ẹni tí gbogbo Ọba ń wò wò, kí wọ́n lè ṣe dáadáa
  • Akọ́ni tí ń ṣàgídígbò ayé
  • Ọba agbaiyé, ẹni tí orun mọ̀, ayé mọ̀
  • Kábíyèsí o, Ọọ̀ni Ilẹ̀-Ifẹ̀!

    • English Meaning

  • Ooni, the divine priest of wisdom who carries sacred destiny
  • King of kings, ruler of the land of Ifẹ̀
  • May your crown never fall
  • Descendant of Odùduwà, Lord of Ilé-Ifẹ̀
  • A king birthed by the earth but fashioned in heaven
  • Native of Ifẹ̀, established firmly in the world
  • Kábíyèsí — unquestionable king, owner of the world
  • Guardian of deities who brings the gods into the palace
  • King who defends his people and never oppresses them
  • Heir of Odùduwà, brave warrior who never fails
  • The king other kings look up to for guidance
  • Mighty ruler who shapes the destiny of the world
  • The king known in heaven and on earth
  • Kábíyèsí o! Ooni of the sacred land of Ifẹ̀!

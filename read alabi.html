ALABI

  • Meaning: One born to the white cloth of Ọbàtálá (deity of creation and purity).Implies purity, piety, and divine favour.
  • Yoruba Oríkì
  • Àlàbí, Ọmọ aláràbà, Ọmọ igi ńlá.
  • Àlàbí, onítẹ́ pẹrẹ́, aláṣọ funfun.
  • A-fín-jù-fín, a-funfun-jù-funfun.
  • Àlàbí, Ọmọ ìwà dáadáa, Ọmọ ìwà fúnfún.
  • A-bú-ni-lọ́rẹ́, a-bùn-ni-lọ́rẹ́-tán.
  • Ọmọ ẹni ọ̀ṣà ń gbà láyé.
  • Ọmọ àràgà-jigijigi.
  • A-fọ́wọ́-wé-wèé-k'olójú-wọ̀nyí-kù

    • English Meaning:

  • Alabi, child of the Iroko tree (lineage name), child of the big tree.
  • Alabi, owner of a wide mat, owner of white clothes.
  • The whitest of the white, the purest of the pure.
  • Alabi, child of good character, child of pure character.
  • One who surprises you with gifts, one who gives gifts completely.
  • Child that the deity saves in the world.
  • The strong, firm, unshakable individual.
  • Child who washes his hands carefully so that the lazy ones fail (proactive and meticulous).

Aina

  • Aina, ọmọ tí a bí pẹ̀lú ayọ̀ àti ìbùkún
  • Ọmọ aláyè gbayì tí gbogbo ilé bọwọ́ fún
  • Ẹni tí Ọlọ́run dá sílẹ̀ fún ìdílé rere
  • Aina, ọmọ tí kì í ṣe burúkú, tí kì í dá ilé láàmú
  • Ọmọ tí orí rẹ pé, ẹni tí gbogbo ènìyàn ń yìn
  • Ẹ̀wẹ̀ tí ń tàn bí ìràwọ̀ ọ̀run
  • Ọmọ rere tí ń ràn gbogbo ilé lọ́wọ́
  • Ọmọ tí a bí láti jẹ́ ìyìn àti àlàáfíà
  • A kì í mọ́ ọ́, ká máa fẹ́ ọ́
  • Aina, ọmọ rere tí Ọlọ́run fi fọwọ́ burúkú dáa
  • Ọmọ tí ayé gbà gẹ́gẹ́ bí ẹ̀bùn Ọlọ́run

    • English Meaning (Aina)

  • Aina, the child born with joy and blessings
  • Noble child respected by all in the household
  • Child created by God for a righteous family
  • Aina, child who does no wrong and brings no harm to the home
  • Child whose destiny is fulfilled and praised by everyone
  • Beauty shining like the stars in the sky
  • Noble child who helps the whole family prosper
  • Child born to bring honor and peace
  • To know you is to love you
  • Aina, the noble child shaped by God’s grace
  • The child the world accepts as a divine gift

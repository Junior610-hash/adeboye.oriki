ORÍKÌ AJÁDÌ (YORÙBÁ)

  • Ajádì,
  • Ọmọ tí kì í jẹ́ kí ogun kó ẹni lọ,
  • Ṣùgbọ́n ó kó ogun lórí,
  • Ó sì fi ọ̀pá ìṣẹ́gun gun gbogbo ibi.
  • Ajádì,
  • Akin tí wọ́n kó nílé ọlá,
  • Ọmọ tí a ní “ẹ̀mí ọba ló mú”
  • Kí o jẹ́ pé o yé ní gbogbo ojú ìjà.
  • Ọmọ tí kì í jẹ́ kí ẹ̀rú wọ inú ọkàn,
  • Ọmọ tí ẹ̀mí ọ̀tá bẹ̀rù bó ti rí.
  • Ajádì,
  • Ọmọ a kínní kára kára,
  • Tí ẹ̀sìn ogun kì í ta lórí rẹ̀,
  • Ọmọ tí Ọlọ́run dá sí orí ayé fún ìṣẹ́gun àti ìbùkún.
  • Ajádì tí orí rẹ̀ kún fún ìbora ọrun,
  • Ọmọ tí ayé kì í pọn yà bí Ọlọ́run ṣe ń fi agbára wọ́ ọ.
  • Ajádì,
  • Ọmọ akíkanjú
  • Tí kò ní sùn lórí ilẹ̀ àìrẹ́,
  • Ọmọ tí a fi ogun kọ́ sílẹ̀
  • Ṣùgbọ́n a fi ìṣẹ́gun kọ̀wé ayé.
  • Kí orí rẹ̀ má bà jé, Kí gbogbo àlá rẹ ṣé, Ajádì, akọni tí kì í yá nígbà ìṣòro!

    ENGLISH MEANING

  • Ajádì,
  • One who refuses to be defeated by war,
  • But instead carries warfare on his shoulders
  • And rides victory like a staff of power.
  • Ajádì,
  • Warrior raised in royalty,
  • One who survives all battles
  • Because a kingly spirit sustains him.
  • One who never allows fear into his heart,
  • Yet fear enters the enemies’ hearts at his sight.
  • Ajádì,
  • A strong and resilient giant,
  • Whom the weapons of war cannot penetrate,
  • Placed on earth by God
    • for victory and blessing.
  • Ajádì whose destiny is shielded by heaven,
  • One whom life cannot bend,
  • Because God clothes him in strength.
  • Ajádì,
  • Brave descendant
  • Who never sleeps on weak ground,
  • Trained not in defeat
  • But in the handwriting of victory.
  • May your destiny remain intact,
  • May all your dreams come true,
  • Ajádì — hero who stands firm in difficult times!

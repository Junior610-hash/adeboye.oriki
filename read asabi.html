Àṣàbí (Asabi)

  • Asabí, ọmọ tí a bí ní ayọ̀, tí a sì tún rí ayọ̀ sí i
  • Ọmọ tí ayé mọ̀ pé a bí ni pátápátá
  • Ẹ̀wẹ̀ ọba tí ń tàn bí ìràwọ̀ ọrun
  • Asabí, ọmọ tí a kì í fi ṣe àṣekára
  • Ẹni tí orí rẹ pé ní ọjọ́ ìbí rẹ̀
  • Ọmọ tí Ọlọ́run da fún ayọ̀ ilé
  • A rí ọ́ ká tún rẹ̀ sùn, ayọ̀ ló jẹ
  • Asabí, ọṣọ ilé tí ń kun ilé sílẹ̀
  • Ọmọ tí àìpẹ̀ rẹ ní ń mú gbogbo ayé yọ̀
  • Ìbímọ tí kò jọ wọ́n ní ìdílé
  • Ọmọ tó tìẹ̀ dà bí adé lórí
  • Ẹ̀wẹ̀ oòrùn tí ń rọ̀ lójú ayé
  • A kì í mọ́ ọ́, ká máa fẹ́ ọ́
  • Asabí, adùn tí a bí, tí Ọlọ́run fi fọwọ́ burúku dáa
  • Ọmọ rere tí kì í jẹ́ kí ìdílé bàjẹ́
  • A kì í ṣe ọ̀kan ṣoṣo, Ọlọ́run ní fi ọ sójú ayé
  • Asabí, ọmọ tí ayé gba gẹ́gẹ́ bí ẹ̀bùn Ọlọ́run

    • English Translation

  • Asabí, the child born in joy and welcomed again with joy
  • A child the world acknowledges as truly and specially born
  • Royal beauty shining like heaven’s stars
  • Asabí, one too precious to be treated casually
  • One whose destiny was perfected from birth
  • Child God created to bring happiness to the home
  • When we see you, we rejoice again — for you are joy
  • Asabí, the ornament that beautifies the family
  • Your presence alone makes the world rejoice
  • A birth unlike any other in the lineage
  • A child whose dignity is like a crown
  • Sunshine pouring brightness upon the earth
  • To know you is to love you
  • Asabí, goodness born and shaped by God’s grace
  • Noble child who keeps the family flourishing
  • One who stands out because God gave you presence
  • Asabí, the child the world accepts as a divine gift

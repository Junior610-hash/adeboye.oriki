ORÍKÌ OLOWO OF OWO

    (Owa / Olowo of Owo — Omo Imade)
  • Olowo ọmọ Imade
  • Ọba aládé tí ń rìn ní ilẹ̀ ìran
  • Omo ẹ̀dá tí orí rẹ̀ pé bí òrùlé ọba
  • A-kì-í rìn sí Owo kí ọlá má bà wa
  • Omo Ọlọ́run tí ilẹ̀ yàn fún ogo
  • Ọba tí ogun ò le ba
  • A-rìn-jọba bí erin
  • Omo tí àjànàkú ń bẹ̀rù
  • Olowo tí ń tàn bí ìmọ́lẹ̀ ọ̀run
  • Omo a pé ní ìlú, a pé ní oko
  • Ọba tí ìrẹ̀pọ̀ ń tẹ̀lé
  • Aláṣẹ lórí ilẹ̀ Ogho tí kò ní parí
  • A-kú-kò-jọba
  • Ọba tí a fi ọwọ́ Ọlọ́run dá sílẹ̀
  • Kí ọlá rẹ̀ ó pé
  • Kí Owo ó ní ìbùkún ilẹ̀ àti ọlá púpọ̀ Ase.

    • Meaning — Olowo of Owo

  • Olowo, child of Imade
  • King whose crown traces ancient royal blood
  • Blessed head that shines like a palace roof
  • Anyone who steps into Owo receives honor
  • Chosen ruler supported by earth and heaven
  • War cannot conquer his throne
  • He rules like a mighty elephant
  • Even the strong fear his command
  • He radiates like heavenly light
  • Blessed in both city and farmland
  • Unity and prosperity walk with him
  • Authority over Owo that shall never fail
  • A king whose legacy outlives death
  • Formed by God’s own hands
  • May his dignity flourish
  • May Owo enjoy endless blessings Ase.

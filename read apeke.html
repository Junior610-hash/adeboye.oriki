Àpèkẹ́ (Apeke)

  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, ọmọ tí a pẹ̀lú rírẹ̀, tí a bọ́wọ̀ fún
  • Ọmọ ayọ̀ tí Ọlọ́run dá sílẹ̀ gẹ́gẹ́ bí adun ìdílé
  • Ẹwà tí ń tàn bí òréré ọ̀sán
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, ọmọ tí ayé fi inú dùn láti ní
  • Ọmọ tí a bá pe, ayé dùn
  • Adùnnú tí a fi ké àbọ̀ fún
  • Ẹja tí kì í ṣe fún gbogbo ènìyàn ní omi ayé
  • Ọmọ a rà lókè, a tún rà lórùn
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́ tí ọkan kò lè ké sí ní kàn
  • Ọmọ tí inú ilé fi ń rọ̀
  • Ẹ̀wẹ̀ tí ń jẹ́ kíkún ọ̀run ayé
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, ọmọ rere tí Ọmọlúwàbí ní
  • Ọrẹ ayé, iranlọwọ tí ń pọ̀ sí ilé
  • A kì í mọ́ ọ́, ká máa fẹ́ ọ́
  • Ẹni tí orí rẹ pé bí adúrà òjíṣẹ́
  • Òòrùn ayé tí ń mú ìmọ̀lára rere bọ̀
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, ọ̀ṣọ̀ tí a ní kí ayé má bàjẹ́

    • English Translation

  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, the one called with tenderness and respect
  • Child of joy created by God as the sweetness of the family
  • Beauty that shines like a bright afternoon
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, the child the world delights to possess
  • When she is called, happiness follows
  • Sweetness wrapped carefully in love
  • A fish that is rare, not for everyone in the river of life
  • One purchased from the heavens and refined on earth
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, a heart no one can condemn
  • Child whose presence brings peace to the household
  • Beauty that completes the harmony of the world
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, noble child of good upbringing
  • Friend of life, blessing increasing the home
  • To know you is to love you
  • The one with a destiny as perfect as answered prayer
  • Sunshine of life that brings good feelings
  • Apẹ̀kẹ́, the ornament we preserve so life remains beautiful

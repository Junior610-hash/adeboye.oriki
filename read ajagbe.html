ORÍKÌ AJÁGBÉ (YORÙBÁ)

  • Ajágbé,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí ogun bá lójú, ó tún dide lọ sí i,
  • Akin tí kì í kúrò lárin ìjà,
  • Ọmọ tí ìdìbọ̀ ogun kì í kọ́ lórí.
  • Ajágbé,
  • Ọmọ tí a bí sí ilé ọlá,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí ọ̀tá bẹ̀rù orúkọ rẹ̀,
  • Kí wọ́n tó rí ojú rẹ̀.
  • Akin, ọmọ àrìnnàkò,
  • Ọmọ tí agbára rẹ̀ kún bí omi inú òkun,
  • Ọmọ tí àṣẹ ń ṣọ́ bí ojú ọba.
  • Ọmọ tí a fi orí ṣé orí,
  • Tí orí rẹ̀ mọ̀ pé ogun ló fi dà,
  • Orí tí a fi wẹ̀ ní ejé àṣẹ,
  • Tí kò ní jẹ́ kí ẹ̀gàn bẹ̀ wọ̀lé.
  • Ajágbé,
  • Kí orí ṣe ọ ní rere,
  • Kí ìṣẹ́gun má bà ọ ní ṣẹ̀sìn,
  • Kí ọlá má bà ẹ lérù.
  • Ọmọ Akinkanju,
  • Gbogbo ibi o bá lọ,
  • Ìbùkún ni wọ́n fi ń pàdé ọ.
  • Ajágbé,
  • Ẹ̀rọ ogun tí kì í ṣubú ní àgbàlá!

    • ENGLISH MEANING

  • Ajágbé,
  • One who sees war and moves closer to it,
  • A warrior who never leaves the battlefield,
  • One whom the chaos of war cannot weaken.
  • Ajágbé,
  • Born into a home of honor,
  • One whose enemies fear his name
  • Before even seeing his face.
  • A heroic traveler,
  • Whose strength is as deep as the ocean,
  • Whose authority shines like a king’s eye.
  • One whose destiny was molded for greatness,
  • Whose head knows war formed him,
  • Washed in the blood of authority
  • So shame will never enter his life.
  • Ajágbé,
  • May destiny always favor you,
  • May victory never abandon you,
  • May honor never become a burden.
  • Warrior child,
  • Wherever you go,
  • Blessings rise to meet you.
  • Ajágbé,
  • War machine that never falls in the courtyard!

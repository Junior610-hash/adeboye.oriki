ORÍKÌ TIMI OF EDE (Oba of Ede — “Timi Agbale Olofa Ina”)

    Agbale Olofa Ina tí í ṣọ Ede bí erin nla
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí ina ò le jo
  • Ọmọ Olúorogun tí ń fo ọ̀fà sílẹ̀ bí ina
  • Omo abẹ́lé tí ẹ̀ṣọ́ kò lè bá jagun
  • Ọba tí Ọlọ́run dá sílẹ̀ lórí ogun
  • A-kì-í sùn lẹ́yìn ogun náà
  • Ọmọ ará Ede tí kì í bọ́ lẹ́yìn nínú ijà
  • Ti kì í jẹ́ kí ọ̀tá bọ láì ru ẹ̀jẹ̀
  • A-gbélé-wo-ru-kò-wó
  • Oba tí ẹ̀rù ò fẹ́ bá sùn
  • A-kún-lójú-kò-bọ̀
  • Ọmọ tí ọlá pọ̀ ju ọrọ̀ lọ
  • Ọba tí Ede ń yìn káàkiri ilú
  • Ọmọ a máa ráyè gbé
  • Mayẹ̀fun, Ọba tí Ajalayé ń bọ́ jẹ
  • Kí orí rẹ̀ ó pé
  • Kí Ede ó tàn ká gbogbo Yorùbá Ase.

    • Meaning — Timi of Ede

  • Timi the warrior whose arrows burn like fire
  • King who protects Ede like a mighty elephant
  • A child whom fire cannot harm
  • Descendant who shoots arrows like blazing flames
  • A ruler whose palace guards can never outfight him
  • A king established by God through war
  • One who never rests after battle
  • He never retreats in conflict
  • He never lets enemies escape without consequence
  • A leader who fears no threat
  • Bold and unshaken in defense
  • A royal whose honor exceeds wealth
  • Greatness follows every step he takes
  • Ede celebrates his glory everywhere
  • A king supported by the living and the ancestors
  • May his reign endure
  • May the light of Ede shine across Yoruba land Ase.

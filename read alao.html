ORÍKÌ ÀSÀMU

  • Àsàmu,
  • Ọmọ ẹ̀dun tí í bẹ̀rù àrọ̀ọ̀rọ̀,
  • Ọmọ akíkanjú tí í kọ’bi sílẹ̀ níbi tí ìkà bá ti bẹ̀rù wá.
  • Tó bá jẹ́ ẹ̀dá míì ni, ogun á ti gbé e lọ,
  • Ṣùgbọ́n ní ọwọ́ Àsàmu, ogun ń sunkún bí ọmọ kékeré.
  • Ọmọ tí kì í bẹ̀rù ẹ̀dá,
  • Ẹni tí ẹ̀dá àti ẹ̀mí ń bẹ̀rù,
  • Ọmọ tí Ọlọ́run fi orí burúkú sẹ́gbẹ̀,
  • Kó fi orí rere bọ̀ sìn bí aṣọ ọba.
  • Àsàmu, ọmọ ọlọ́la tí wọ̩n mọ̀ sínú ọlá,
  • Ọmọ tí a kì í ṣe nílẹ̀ ẹrú,
  • Ọmọ tí a fi ọlá gọ́,
  • Ọmọ tí a fi ìbùkún fọ́.
  • Ọmọ gúnugún tí kì í bẹ̀rù apá,
  • Ọmọ ẹkùn tí kì í rìn bójú sẹ́yìn,
  • Ọmọ tí a kì í fi jẹ́ kí ajá ọ̀tá bù lu.
  • Àsàmu tí orí rẹ̀ ní ìtàn asẹ̀,
  • Ẹ̀mí tí ìṣẹ́gun bo lórí bi aṣọ àlà dudarà,
  • Ọmọ tí Ọlọ́run dá sí orí ayé fún àṣẹ àti àlàáfíà.
  • Àsàmu, ẹni tí adúrà ló yá sílẹ̀,
  • Ọmọ tí kì í bẹ̀rù òru,
  • Ẹ̀mí tí kì í sunkún ní wákàtí ìjà.
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí ohun tí ó bá bá nira lórí,
  • Ó ma yipada sí ìrọ̀rùn ní ọwọ́ rẹ̀.
  • Àsàmu,
  • Kí orí mú ọ rìn ní rere,
  • Kí olódùmarè gbà ẹ́,
  • Kí ọlá má bà ọ ní jẹ,
  • Kí ayé jẹ́ alàáfíà sí ọ ní gbogbo ọjọ́ ayé rẹ.
  • Àsàmu ọmọ akíkanjú,
  • Akin ni wọ́n bí,
  • Akin ni wọ́n kọ́,
  • Akin ni ìkẹyìn ti ìran rẹ̀.

    • FULL ENGLISH MEANING

  • Àsàmu,
  • Child of the thunderous beast feared by deep darkness,
  • Warrior child who stands boldly where wickedness retreats.
  • If he were anyone else, battle would have swallowed him,
  • But in Àsàmu’s hands, battle cries like a small child.
  • Child who fears no being,
  • A being both humans and spirits fear,
  • One whom God removed misfortune from his head,
  • And clothed his destiny in a royal garment.
  • Àsàmu, noble-born wrapped in royalty,
  • Never born into slavery,
  • Born with honor,
  • Washed in blessings.
  • Descendant of the eagle unfearful of wings,
  • Child of the lion who never walks backwards,
  • One who never allows the enemy dog to bark in victory.
  • Àsàmu whose destiny carries authority,
  • A spirit covered in victory like a beautiful royal cloth,
  • One whom God placed upon the earth for leadership and peace.
  • Àsàmu, the one set apart by prayer,
  • Fearless one in the night,
  • A spirit that does not cry in the hour of war.
  • A child who turns difficult situations
  • Into ease and triumph.
  • Àsàmu,
  • May destiny lead you in goodness,
  • May the Almighty support you,
  • May honor never fail you,
  • May life remain peaceful to you every day.
  • Àsàmu, descendant of warriors,
  • A warrior born,
  • A warrior trained,
  • And a warrior forever in his bloodline.

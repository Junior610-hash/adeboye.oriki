ALAKE

  • Aláké, ọba tí a ń ké orúkọ rẹ ní gbogbo ayé
  • Ọmọ a rà lóòkè, a tún rà lélẹ̀
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí orí rẹ pé, tí ayé gbà gbọ́
  • Ajíṣe bí Ọlọ́run, ẹni tí a ṣe dáradára
  • A kì í kọ́ Ọmọ Aláké sílẹ̀, ayé á dákẹ́ gbọ́
  • Ọmọ tí a bí sípò, tí ipò sì tún dáradára sí i
  • Ọmọ aláṣẹ, ọmọ adé kì í rọ̀
  • Ọba tí wọ́n bí láyé tí wọ́n tún ṣe ní ọ̀run
  • Aláké, ọmọ akọni tí kì í sá fún ogun ayé
  • Ológo tí ń tàn bí ìràwọ̀ ọrun
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí gbogbo ayé ń bẹ̀rù ìbínú rẹ
  • Ṣàngó ti ara àtààrò, akíkanjú ọmọ ogun
  • Ẹnì tí a ké lẹ́nu, tí a sì tún ké lẹ́kàn
  • Ọmọ tí Ọlọ́run ní, ayé sì gbìn
  • Aláké, ọmọ rere tí kì í ṣeré burúkú
  • Ọrẹ ayé, ayọ̀ ilé, asọ̀ lórí ìdílé
  • Kábíyèsí o, Aláké, ọba tí a ń yin lógun

    • English Translation

  • Aláké, the royal one whose name is shouted in praise everywhere
  • Child who is valued both from the heavens and on earth
  • Child with a perfect destiny accepted by the world
  • One made beautifully by God
  • The child that cannot be abandoned, for the world will become silent
  • One born into honor who keeps rising in honor
  • Child of authority, whose crown never falls
  • A king born on earth and crowned again in heaven
  • Aláké, child of warriors who never flee from life’s battle
  • Glorious one shining like the stars in the sky
  • Child whose anger the world fears
  • The thunderous one, brave descendant of powerful ancestors
  • One called out loudly and called again
  • The child who belongs to God and is planted firmly in the world
  • Aláké, the good child who does not misuse goodness
  • Friend of life, joy of the home, the pride of the family
  • We hail you, Aláké, king worthy of honor

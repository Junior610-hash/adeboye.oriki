ORÍKÌ DEJI OF AKURE

    (Oba of Akure — Omo Asodeboyede)
  • Omo Asodeboyede
  • Oba a bi ní àga ọba mọ̀
  • Ọmọ Olókìtì tí kì í ṣe ọmọ alákìtàn
  • Ọba tí ń jẹ́ kó yẹ kí Akure lórí
  • A-rìn-ń-jọba
  • Ọba tí a bí lómùgàn
  • Ọmọ ti kì í jẹ́ kí ẹ̀fúùrù gbe orúkọ Akure lọ
  • Aládé tí ń ṣan bí oòrùn ọ̀sán
  • Oba tí òun ni ibùkún ilẹ̀
  • A kì í rìn s’Akure kí ire má bà wa
  • Ọmọ tí a fi orí ṣe ọlá
  • Ọba tí gbogbo ọ̀tá bẹ̀rù rẹ̀
  • Ọmọ Olókò tí ń dá omi duro
  • Ọba tí ń dákẹ́ bí òkúta, tí gbogbo ayé bẹ̀rù
  • Akure agbègbè tí ọlá ń wọ̀
  • Kí orí rẹ̀ ó pé
  • Kí Akure ó la gbogbo ìnira já Ase.

    • Meaning — Deji of Akure

  • Child of Asodeboyede
  • A king born directly on the throne
  • Descendant of nobility, not of lowliness
  • A leader who elevates Akure’s prestige
  • A ruler whose steps command authority
  • Born into greatness and divine guardianship
  • One who protects the name of Akure from disgrace
  • A shining crown like the afternoon sun
  • He is the blessing of the land
  • Whoever arrives in Akure receives goodness
  • Crowned with enduring honor
  • Even enemies fear his presence
  • A royal of the river and land
  • Silent in power like a mighty stone
  • Akure flourishes under his reign
  • May his crown endure
  • May Akure overcome every hardship Ase.

ORÍKÌ Ọ̀ṢOGBO (Osogbo Praise Poetry)

  • Ọ̀ṣogbo Ọ̀ròkì tí a ò ṣeré mọ́
  • Ilé tí Ọlọ́run ti wà gbọ́dọ̀
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo tí a fi ilẹ̀ ọlá dá
  • Ilé tí orò kì í wọ̀
  • A kì í ṣeré nílé Ọ̀ṣogbo kó ní di ọ̀rọ̀
  • Ọmọ Oso tí kò ṣeré lọ́wọ́ ẹ̀dá
  • Ilé ìgbàgbọ́ tí Ọ̀ṣun dá sílẹ̀
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo alákìísa ọlá
  • A rí ire gbogbo níbẹ̀
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo ọmọ a tàn ré aṣọ dúdú
  • A ṣeré jẹ̀jẹ̀ bí Ọmọ ẹni
  • Omọ Òba Ataoja tí Ọ̀sun fi adé lórí
  • Abẹ́lé tí a fi oyè jẹ
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo tí a mọ̀ ní ìlú aláàárù
  • Àmọ́ nígbà ìjà, ọmọ jagun jagun
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo tí ò ní ká ṣeré sí orí omi
  • Bí omi ṣe wà, bẹẹ́ ni ire á bọ̀
  • Ilé tí ogún ò le bá jẹ́
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo tí o wà larin àgùnbánirọ
  • Tí ògbọ́n ń jọ sílẹ̀ lojoojúmọ́
  • Ará Ọ̀ṣogbo ọmọ a bọ̀ mí ní ìbòwò
  • Tí ọ̀tá bá dé, a máa fọ́mọ bọ́lá
  • Ọ̀ṣogbo tí Ọlọ́run kò fi ṣe èrín
  • Ilé tí orí kì í ṣì
  • Ilé tí ìbùkún ń jó
  • Kí o ma bàjẹ́ nígbà gbogbo Aṣẹ.

    • Meaning in English – Praise of Osogbo

  • Osogbo Oroki, where there is no room for frivolity
  • A city established by divine authority
  • A town formed upon a foundation of honor
  • Where evil does not enter
  • One must tread carefully in Osogbo or face consequences
  • Descendants of mystical heritage, not given to carelessness
  • City of spiritual strength founded by Oshun
  • Osogbo, richly adorned with dignity
  • A place where good fortune abounds
  • Osogbo, whose people dress beautifully in deep cloth
  • Gentle in peace, yet noble in pride
  • Home of Ataoja, crowned by sacred blessing
  • A dynasty respected from generation to generation
  • Osogbo — known as a community of artisans
  • Yet fearless when war arises
  • A city that does not trifle with river waters
  • Where water runs, blessings flow
  • A home untouched by destruction
  • Osogbo surrounded by hills of strength
  • Where wisdom is born daily
  • Children of Osogbo honor and protect their own
  • Enemies come and flee in fear
  • Osogbo — never a place for mockery
  • A city where destiny does not fail
  • A land where blessings dance freely
  • May you prosper forever Ase.

ORÍKÌ ÀRÍYỌ̀ (YORÙBÁ)

  • Àríyọ̀,
  • Èyí tí wọ́n bí, ayọ̀ ni wọ́n kẹ́,
  • Ọmọ tí ayọ̀ rẹ̀ kọjú sùúrù ayé,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí ayọ̀ wà níbìkan tí ó bá wọlé.
  • Àríyọ̀, ọmọ ire gbogbo,
  • Ẹni tí ayé fi ayọ̀ bọ lórí ìrora,
  • Ọmọ tí a ní “àlááfíà ló dé”.
  • Ẹ̀mí ayọ̀ tí kì í bọ́kẹ̀kẹ́,
  • Ọmọ tí ayọ̀ rẹ̀ ń rìn bí ẹyẹ alárìnkiri.
  • Ẹni tí kì í ṣe níbi tí ayọ̀ ò wà,
  • Ọmọ tí kì í jẹ́ kí ayọ̀ ọkàn di pẹtẹlẹ.
  • Àríyọ̀,
  • Kí ayọ̀ má bà ọ ní jẹ́,
  • Kí ayọ̀ má bà ọ ní pé,
  • Kí ayọ̀ má bà ọ ní lórí kúrò.
  • Ọmọ tí ayọ̀ rẹ̀ wà ní ọwọ́ Ọlọ́run,
  • Ọmọ aláyọ̀ ṣoṣo,
  • Omo Adéayọ̀ tó ní ayọ̀ ní gbogbo ọjọ́.
  • Àríyọ̀ tí ayọ̀ rẹ̀ jẹ́ ayérayé!

    • ENGLISH MEANING — ÀRÍYỌ̀

  • Àríyọ̀,
  • The one whose birth brought celebration,
  • A child whose joy silences the impatience of life,
  • One who brings joy wherever he enters.
  • Àríyọ̀, child of all goodness,
  • One whose arrival turned pain into joy,
  • A child known as “peace has come.”
  • A spirit of joy that never fades,
  • A child whose happiness flies like a free bird.
  • One who is never found where joy does not exist,
  • A child who never allows joy in the heart to be destroyed.
  • Àríyọ̀,
  • May joy never fail you,
  • May joy never delay for you,
  • May joy never depart from your life.
  • A child whose joy is placed in God’s hands,
  • Bearer of everlasting happiness,
  • Royal offspring whose days overflow with joy.
  • Àríyọ̀ whose joy is eternal!

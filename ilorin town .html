ORÍKÌ ÌLỌ́RIN — Ilorin Town Praise Poetry

  • Ìlọ́rin ofòwódìmọ̀
  • Ìlọ́rin ọ̀tẹ̀jẹ̀-nlá tí wọ́n fi òkúta ṣe ìlú
  • Ilé tí ọ̀pá kì í bàjẹ́
  • Ìlọ́rin tí a fi ọgbọ́n ṣe ọlá
  • Ìlọ́rin tí ogun kì í gba
  • Òkè Sálámì níbí tí ọba ń bò wá
  • Ìlọ́rin ọmọ Àfonjá Ọmọ ará Ilé-Gbònnà
  • Ọmọ tí ogun kì í bori, ìjà kì í le
  • lọ́rin Ilé tí Àlùfáà ń jogún
  • Ilé tí ìmàmù ń jẹ̀yìn
  • Ilé tí a fi àdúrà ṣe ogiri
  • lọ́rin tí irọ̀kò ò gbọ́
  • Ìlú tí wọ́n fi orúkọ Ọlọ́hun kọ́
  • Ará Ìlọ́rin ọmọ a máyègun
  • Ọmọ ọdẹ tí ń ṣeré ní ẹ̀bọra
  • Ọmọ tí ogun kì í lé ní ilé
  • Tí ọ̀tá bá dé, a fẹ̀yà jẹ
  • A b'ọta jẹ́ jẹ́bi
  • Ìlọ́rin Ilé tí ọlá ń kúró nínú omi
  • Ọmọ alágniyàn tó mọ bí a ṣe ń tàn
  • Ìlọ́rin ò le bàjẹ́
  • Ìlọ́rin Ilé ọba tí a kì í gbà ẹrú lọ
  • Ọmọ tí Ọlọ́run fún ní ọlá
  • Kí Ilọ́rin ó yọ̀ bí ìrì
  • Kí ìbùkún rẹ̀ máa pọ̀ si i Ase.
    • Meaning in English — Ilorin Praise
  • Ilorin, city of strength and dignity
  • Mighty city built with stones of courage
  • A land where pillars never decay
  • Where wisdom is the foundation of honor
  • Ilorin that warfare cannot defeat
  • Land of Afonja the great warrior
  • A people unconquered, fearless in battle
  • Ilorin, city inherited by scholars and clerics
  • Where Imams reign with knowledge and prayer
  • A city whose walls are fortified with faith
  • A place established under God’s divine name
  • People of Ilorin, champions in every victory
  • Warriors who triumph through strategy
  • Enemies come and are scattered in shame
  • Ilorin, where greatness flows like water
  • Land of brilliant minds and cunning strength
  • If they say a town is ruined,
  • Ilorin can never fall
  • Ilorin, the royal city that frees and uplifts
  • Gifted honor by the Almighty
  • May Ilorin flourish like the early dew
  • May blessings continually increase upon the land

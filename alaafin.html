Alaafin of Oyo (Aláàfin ti Òyó)

  • Alaafin Ọyọ,
  • Kabiyesi, Alasè Ẹ̀kẹ́jẹ̀ Ọ̀run,
  • Ọba ti ki i jẹ́ kó rọ̀ mọ́lẹ̀, kó má jẹjẹ́ mọ́lẹ̀,
  • Ọmọ Ọranyan, ọmọ Odùduwà,
  • Àwọn tí wọ́n njọba ní Ọ̀yọ́ tí ilẹ̀ ń gbìmọ̀ sí,
  • Aláṣẹ ti ń pa aṣẹ run, tí kò sì gba aṣẹ kúrò lọ́wọ́ ẹnikẹ́ni,
  • Ọ̀kan ṣoṣo tí Olorun fi fún láṣẹ lórí gbogbo Yorùbá,
  • K’áto rí Ọyalẹ̀, ọdẹ̀ tí ń balẹ̀ f’ọba.
  • Ọba tí inú rẹ̀ kò ń bàjẹ́,
  • Tí àwọn ọtá rẹ̀ fi ń bẹ̀rù rẹ̀ bí iná,
  • Ọmọ tí Ọ̀yọ́ ò mọ ẹni tí ó bi i,
  • Oba tí ń jẹun nílé ayé,
  • Tí ń jẹun nílé ọ̀run.
  • Alaafin,
  • Kábíyèsí ọba ti Mọ́lẹ̀bí ń fẹ́,
  • Ọba tí ẹ̀mí ló fi ń pa nílé,
  • Ọmọ tí ilẹ̀ kò ní pa,
  • Baba Ọyọ́ ti ilẹ̀ ń gbé lórí.
  • Alaafin Ọyọ,
  • Ọba Ajíkí òun aṣọ tó fi wọ̀ lọ sí igbó,
  • A kì í rí ìbànújẹ́ nílé Alaafin,
  • Bí kò bá ṣẹ̀yìn, ó ní ó ṣẹ̀yìn ní ọ̀la.

    • English Translation (Meaning)

  • Alaafin of Oyo,
  • The unquestionable king, ruler of the visible and invisible world,
  • The king whose authority cannot be undermined,
  • Child of Oranyan, descendant of Oduduwa,
  • He who reigns in Oyo where the land itself gives counsel,
  • The supreme authority who destroys all rival commands,
  • The one whom God has given power over all Yoruba land,
  • Before anyone sees Oyalẹ̀ (the royal guards),
  • The hunter already bows before the king.
  • The king whose heart does not break,
  • Whose enemies fear like fierce fire,
  • A child whose origin Oyo itself cannot deny,
  • A king who eats from the riches of the earth
  • And feasts also with the heavens.
  • Alaafin,
  • Your majesty, beloved by the royal lineage,
  • The king who slays with command alone,
  • The child that the earth itself cannot defeat,
  • Father of Oyo, whom the land upholds with respect.
  • Alaafin of Oyo,
  • A king who wakes with greatness and wears honor like a garment,
  • There is no sorrow in the house of the Alaafin,
  • If today disappoints, tomorrow will certainly bring blessings.

ORÍKÌ ALÁRA ARÁMỌ́KỌ́ — King of Aramoko-Ekiti

  • Alára Aramoko
  • Ọmo Alákànjù tó gbé òun mọ́ ogun
  • Ọmọ Èkìtì tí inú àgọ̀ jagun ló ti wá
  • Ọba tí kò mọ̀ ẹ̀rù ẹnìkan
  • A-sọ̀rọ̀-sọ̀rọ̀ níwájú ọ̀tá
  • Akìkanjú ní ilé, jagun níta
  • Ọmọ ará Òkè Arámọ́kọ́ tí a bí ní kúrù-kúrù ogun
  • Tí a jó bí esin
  • A-kú-kúrò-lórí-apáta
  • Ọmọ ti Ọlọ́run gbé yọ̀ sí ibi ọlá
  • A-bẹ̀ni kúrò ní ìpò kéré sí ipò ayéremí
  • Ọba tí ó ní orí kíkún ayé
  • Omo a sáré lọ sí ibi tí ogun wà
  • Ẹ yẹ ẹ́ síwájú kí ẹ̀yin ó lè yọ̀ lẹ́yìn
  • A jẹ́ arìnrìn-àjò, ẹni tí gbogbo ilẹ̀ ń bọ́ kí ó tó dé
  • Alára, Ọmọ Àrà
  • mo aláradá tí kì í ṣe ìránṣẹ́ ẹlòmíì
  • A máa kọ ilé, a tún á pa ogun jẹ
  • A kì í ṣe ọmọ tí ó gbọ́ ọrọ̀ ìbànújẹ̀ kí ó bẹ̀rù
  • Olúkùú lúborí
  • A kì í se ẹni tí ẹ̀rù ń pa lọ́run
  • Ọba tí ọlá rẹ̀ ń tan ní gbogbo Ìlú Èkìtì
  • Kí Ọlọ́run ó sọ orí rẹ̀ di dígidigi
  • Kí Aramoko lè bọ́ látọ́run sí ayé ìṣọ̀kan Ase.

    • English Interpretation

  • Alara, the brave king of Aramoko
  • Child of warriors raised in the camp of battle
  • A king who fears no human being
  • He whose words strike like arrows before enemies
  • Gentle at home but fierce in war
  • Born in the midst of conflict like a rushing horse
  • The one who survives hardship and rises from the rock to glory
  • Lifted by God from a low place to the throne
  • The ruler blessed with fullness of destiny
  • He runs into danger so his people can remain safe
  • A great traveler whose arrival brings honor to lands
  • Alara, descendant of Àrà — noble and independent
  • Builder of cities, conqueror in war
  • He does not tremble at bad news
  • The crowned head who always triumphs
  • A king whose honor spreads across Ekiti
  • May God strengthen your crown forever
  • And let Aramoko prosper in unity and peace Ase.

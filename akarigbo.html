ORÍKÌ AKARIGBO OF REMO / SAGAMU

    (Akarigbo of Ìjẹ̀bú-Remo / Sàgámù Royal Throne)
  • Akarigbo ọmọ Ìrèmòjẹ̀
  • Ọba tí a bí ní ọlá
  • A-fọwọ́-kàn-òun-gbà
  • Ọmọ tí ilẹ̀ Remo fi orí sojú
  • Akarigbo tí ń jẹ́ kó yẹ kí Remo lórí
  • Omo ẹni tí kì í jẹ́ kí ẹ̀gún gba ilẹ̀
  • A-ra-ń-jọba nípò ọ̀run
  • Ọba tí inú ilẹ̀ ń bọ́ sílẹ̀ sí
  • Omo Onipopo tí ń yin baba wọ̀
  • Ọba tí kì í fi Remo sẹ́yìn
  • Alákóso gbogbo Remo tí ire ń tẹ̀lé
  • Ọba tí oògùn ò le ṣe bí ara rẹ̀
  • Akarigbo tí orí rẹ̀ kún fún ọlá
  • Ọmọ a-kú-kò-jọba
  • Kí ori ọba won ó pé
  • Kí Remo ó gbé orúkọ rẹ̀ sókè Ase.

    • Meaning — Akarigbo of Remo

  • Akarigbo, pride of the Remo clans
  • A king birthed into royal privilege
  • Authority flows from his very touch
  • Remo stands boldly by his crown
  • A ruler who uplifts his kingdom
  • He leaves no room for evil to claim the land
  • His kingship is ordained by the heavens
  • The earth rejoices beneath his feet
  • Descendant who reveres his ancestors
  • Defender who never forsakes Remo people
  • Leader of all Remo — followed by prosperity
  • No power can rival his divine essence
  • His crown overflows with glory
  • A king whose name outlives death
  • May he reign long
  • May Remo uphold his greatness forever Ase.

ORÍKÌ ASEYIN OF ISEYIN

    (King of Ìṣẹ̀yìn — Omo Aseyin Ìbàdànlé)
  • Aseyin, Ọba tí ń jẹ Iseyin lógùn
  • Òrìṣà tí ń gbé ní agbègbè Oke Odo
  • Omo ẹni tí àfònrọ̀ ò lè gbé lọ
  • Oba tí a bí lórí àgọ́ Ajẹ́
  • Aseyin ọmọ Èkejì-Olu
  • Ọmọ alágbára tí ń ṣáyẹ̀bíjọ
  • Omo tí Iseyin fi orí balẹ̀
  • A kì í bu Aseyin ní ọrọ̀, kí a má fọ irúbo
  • Aláṣẹ lórí ilẹ̀ oyè tí ò le yí padà
  • Ọba tí kì í jẹ́ kí olè rẹ̀ bwo ará
  • Omo a mú ìlú sọ̀kan
  • Aseyin ẹni tí gbogbo agbègbè bo wo wò
  • Ọba tí Ọlọ́run fi f’ogun jẹ
  • Omo a kúnlẹ̀ tí ọlá ń gòkè
  • Kí orí rẹ̀ ó pé
  • Kí Iseyin ó tàn ká gbogbo ayé Ase.

    • Meaning — Aseyin of Iseyin

  • Aseyin, king whose authority is strength to Iseyin
  • A revered spirit of the Oke Odo region
  • A descendant no wind can uproot
  • A ruler born from sacred warrior heritage
  • Heir of powerful lineage
  • One who brings celebration and unity
  • Crowned by ancestral prestige
  • No one speaks against Aseyin without consequence
  • Authority that cannot be reversed
  • Protector who shields his people from harm
  • He who unites kingdoms under peace
  • Aseyin whose presence inspires honor everywhere
  • A king established by divine hands
  • One whose head radiates dignity
  • May his reign endure
  • May Iseyin shine across the world Ase.

ORÍKÌ AKÁNJÍ (YORÙBÁ)

  • Akánjí,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí a bí, wọ́n ní “a kú òkùnkùn”,
  • Ó dáhùn pé, “Èmi ni mo tì dé”,
  • Ọmọ akíkanjú tí ń bá oró ṣeré bí ó ti lè wu.
  • Akánjí,
  • Akin tí kì í bẹ̀rù kú,
  • Ọmọ tí ogun pọ̀ sílẹ̀, ó kọ̀wé ìṣẹ́gun sí orí ẹ̀.
  • Ori burúkú ò lè fi ọwọ́ kan ọ,
  • Ori rere ló wọ lórí rẹ̀ láti ibi oyun wá.
  • Ọmọ ìdí tí kì í sùn,
  • Ọmọ ẹni tí a ṣe ní àkúnya ọkùnrin,
  • Akin tí a fi aya obìnrin kọ́.
  • Akánjí,
  • Tí kì í bẹ̀rù Ẹ̀mí,
  • Ẹ̀mí náà ló bẹ̀rù rẹ.
  • Akin tí kì í ṣubú sálẹ̀,
  • Ọmọ tí a gbé sókè tí Ọlọ́run ń gbé soke sí i.
  • Kí orí rẹ̀ má bà jé,
  • Kí ìṣẹ́gun má bà ọ ní ṣẹ̀sìn,
  • Kí ayé má bà ọ ní ṣẹ̀sìn.
  • Akánjí ọmọ akíkanjú,
  • Akin la bí,
  • Akin la kọ́,
  • Àmúró tí kì í fọ́ ní ọjọ́ ogun!

    • ENGLISH MEANING — AKÁNJÍ

  • Akánjí,
  • Child born and darkness was told “we conquer you,”
  • And he responded, “I have arrived with strength.”
  • A fearless one who enjoys challenges no matter how tough.
  • Akánjí,
  • A warrior unafraid of death,
  • One who writes victory on the battlefield.
  • Misfortune cannot touch your head,
  • You were crowned with a good destiny from the womb.
  • Child of sleepless determination,
  • Formed with the strength of a man,
  • Trained with the heart of a courageous woman.
  • Akánjí,
  • Who fears no spirit,
  • But spirits fear him.
  • A warrior who refuses to fall,
  • One whom God lifts higher and higher.
  • May your destiny never spoil,
  • May victory never forsake you,
  • May life always favor you.
  • Akánjí, descendant of warriors,
  • A warrior born,
  • A warrior trained,
  • A warrior who never breaks in battle!
